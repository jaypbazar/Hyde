HYDE is an educational tool designed to simulate an evil twin attack. It demonstrates how hackers can easily create fake Wi-Fi networks to intercept and potentially steal sensitive information. This device is intended solely for educational purposes. It should never be used to harm or damage any person, device, or network. By understanding how these attacks work, you can take steps to protect yourself and your devices from cyber threats.
